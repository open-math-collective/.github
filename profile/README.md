# Open Math Collectvie

We are a non-profit, engineering-focused initiative dedicated to building free, high-quality educational tools.

## Philosophy

All our tools and APIs are, `and always will be` free and open-source, ensuring equal educational opportunities for everyone.


## Contributing

We welcome contributions from developers interested in our mission - educational technology and algorithmic math.

If you are interested in joining our pack - send an email to the [maintainer](https://github.com/szymonwilczek): szymonwilczek@outlook.com

Please, place in the title `[OMC]` as it will be easier to find that in the mailbox.
